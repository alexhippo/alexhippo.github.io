How to play

Tic-tac-toe (also known as noughts and crosses) is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in the grid below. The players in this game will be you and the computer.

The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
